## Why Convert GIF to MP4?

- **File size.** MP4 is typically 10–20x smaller than GIF at similar visual quality.
- **Quality.** H.264 supports millions of colors; GIF is limited to 256. Converting improves quality and reduces size.
- **Compatibility.** MP4 plays on every modern device and in social platforms that limit or downscale GIF.
- **Performance.** Smaller files mean faster loading and less bandwidth.

Doing this conversion **client side** and **browser local** keeps your clips on your device—**no upload 2026**, so no privacy risk.
