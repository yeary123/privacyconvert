**AVIF** and **PNG** solve different problems. Here’s when to use each and how to convert privately.

## AVIF in 2026

- **Smaller size** — Often 50%+ smaller than JPEG at similar quality.
- **Wide support** — Browsers, Android, Apple; editing support is growing.
- **Good for** — Photos, web assets, storage savings.

## When to use PNG

- **Lossless** — No generation loss; ideal for UI, screenshots, graphics.
- **Transparency** — Alpha channel; AVIF supports it too but PNG is universal.
- **Compatibility** — Every platform and tool opens PNG.

- **AVIF** — Smaller, great for photos and web.
- **PNG** — Lossless, transparent, universal.
