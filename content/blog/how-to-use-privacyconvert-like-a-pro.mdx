# How to Use PrivacyConvert Like a Pro

PrivacyConvert is a **privacy-first file converter** that runs entirely in your browser—your files are never uploaded to our servers. Whether you use the free tier or Pro, a few habits help you get the most out of it. This guide covers workflows, Pro benefits, and how we compare to tools like Convertio, VERT.sh, and localconvert.

![PrivacyConvert runs conversion in your browser; no upload required](https://picsum.photos/800/450?random=8)

## Core Idea: Everything Stays Local

Every conversion runs in your browser. Your file is never sent to our servers. In practice:

- **First visit:** The page may load the converter engine (e.g. FFmpeg.wasm). This is a one-time download and cache; your data is not uploaded.
- **Every conversion:** You select or drag a file; conversion runs in the tab; you download the result. All of it stays on your device.

Compared with Convertio, we never see your file. Compared with VERT.sh and localconvert, we share the same local-first approach and add more formats plus a clear Pro tier.

## Step-by-Step: Any Converter

1. **Open the right tool.** Use [Tools](/tools) or go directly to [AVIF to PNG](/convert/avif-to-png), [WebP to PNG](/convert/webp-to-png), [WAV to MP3](/convert/wav-to-mp3), [OGG to MP3](/convert/ogg-to-mp3), [GIF to MP4](/convert/gif-to-mp4), [MP4 to WebM](/convert/mp4-to-webm), [PNG to JPEG](/convert/png-to-jpeg), or [PDF to Images](/convert/pdf-to-images) (Pro).
2. **Load the converter (if prompted).** Click “Load” once. The engine downloads and caches; your file is not uploaded.
3. **Add your file(s).** Drag and drop or click. Free: one file; Pro: multiple files for batch.
4. **Wait for conversion.** Processing is **client side**. Don’t close the tab until done.
5. **Download.** Save the output. The whole flow stays on your device.

## Pro Tips for Speed and Reliability

- **Keep the tab open.** After loading the engine once, leave the converter tab open for multiple conversions so you don’t reload every time.
- **Use a modern browser.** Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari with WebAssembly work best.
- **Reasonable file size.** For very large files (e.g. long WAVs or big video), consider splitting or converting in smaller batches so the browser stays responsive.
- **Bookmark your favorite converters.** Quick links to [AVIF to PNG](/convert/avif-to-png), [WAV to MP3](/convert/wav-to-mp3), [WebP to PNG](/convert/webp-to-png) speed up repeat use.

## FAQ

**Do I need an account to use PrivacyConvert?**  
No. Free single-file conversion works without an account. Pro is for batch and extra features; conversion still runs in your browser.

**Is the “Load converter” step uploading my file?**  
No. Only the converter code (e.g. FFmpeg.wasm) is downloaded. Your files are never sent to us.

**What’s the difference vs Convertio?**  
Convertio uploads your file to their servers. PrivacyConvert runs conversion entirely in your browser.

**How does it compare to VERT.sh and localconvert?**  
Like them, we keep conversion local. We offer more formats and a clear Pro tier.

**Can I use it offline after loading?**  
The converter code is cached, but the page itself usually needs a first load online. After that, conversion runs in the tab with no need to send your file.

**What does Pro add?**  
Unlimited batch, PDF to Images, and other features. All conversion still runs in your browser.

**Which formats are supported?**  
AVIF, WebP, PNG, JPEG, WAV, MP3, OGG, MP4, WebM, GIF, and PDF-to-images (Pro). See [Tools](/tools) or the converter pages above.

**Is it really completely local?**  
Yes. Your file is read in the browser, processed in the tab, and the result is offered for download. Nothing is sent to our servers.
