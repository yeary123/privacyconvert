## Core Idea: No Upload, Completely Local

Every conversion on PrivacyConvert runs in your browser. Your file is never sent to our servers. That’s **no upload 2026** and **client side** by design. So:

- **First visit:** The page may load the converter engine (e.g. FFmpeg.wasm). This is a one-time download and cache; your data is not uploaded.
- **Every conversion:** You select or drag a file; conversion runs in the tab; you download the result. All **browser local** and **completely local**.

Vs Convertio, we never see your file. Vs VERT.sh and vs localconvert, we share the same local approach and add more tools and a clear Pro tier for 2026.

## Step-by-Step: Any Converter

1. **Open the right tool.** Use [Tools](/tools) or go directly to [AVIF to PNG](/convert/avif-to-png), [WebP to PNG](/convert/webp-to-png), [WAV to MP3](/convert/wav-to-mp3), [OGG to MP3](/convert/ogg-to-mp3), [GIF to MP4](/convert/gif-to-mp4), [MP4 to WebM](/convert/mp4-to-webm), [PNG to JPEG](/convert/png-to-jpeg), or [PDF to Images](/convert/pdf-to-images) (Pro).
2. **Load the converter (if prompted).** Click “Load” once. The engine downloads and caches; your file is not uploaded.
3. **Add your file(s).** Drag and drop or click. Free: one file; Pro: multiple files for batch.
4. **Wait for conversion.** Processing is **client side**. Don’t close the tab until done.
5. **Download.** Save the output. Entire flow is **no upload 2026** and **completely local**.

## Pro Tips for Speed and Reliability

- **Keep the tab open.** After loading the engine once, leave the converter tab open for multiple conversions so you don’t reload every time.
- **Use a modern browser.** Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari with WebAssembly work best. Keeps **client side** conversion smooth.
- **Reasonable file size.** For very large files (e.g. long WAVs or big video), consider splitting or converting in smaller batches so the **browser local** process stays responsive.
- **Bookmark your favorite converters.** Quick access to [AVIF to PNG](/convert/avif-to-png), [WAV to MP3](/convert/wav-to-mp3), [WebP to PNG](/convert/webp-to-png) makes **no upload 2026** workflows faster.
