What makes a **privacy-first file converter** in 2026? One that never sees your files.

## No upload vs cloud

| | Cloud (e.g. Convertio) | No-upload (e.g. PrivacyConvert, VERT.sh) |
|--|------------------------|------------------------------------------|
| Where files go | Their servers | Your browser only |
| Account needed | Often yes | No (free); Pro for extras |
| Speed | Depends on upload | Starts immediately |
| Privacy | They can log/scan | They never receive data |

## What to look for

- **100% client-side conversion** — FFmpeg.wasm or similar in the tab.
- **No tracking on the conversion path** — No pixels or scripts that see your file.
- **Clear pricing** — Free single-file; Pro for batch/history if you want to support the project.

## Best privacy converter in 2026

The “best” is the one that fits your workflow and never uploads. PrivacyConvert offers AVIF, WebP, PNG, WAV, MP3, OGG, GIF, MP4, WebM, and more — all in the browser.
